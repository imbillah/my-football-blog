Winning Celebration

The 2021 Copa América Final was a football match to determine the winners of the 2021 Copa América. The match was the 47th final of the Copa América, a quadrennial tournament contested by the men's national teams of the member associations of CONMEBOL. The match was held at Maracanã Stadium in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil on 10 July 2021.The match featured two rival teams: Argentina and the tournament hosts, Brazil. Argentina won the match 1–0
